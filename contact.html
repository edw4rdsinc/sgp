Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?▶

As soon as you have your date. Popular weekends in summer book 2-3 months ahead. For larger festivals, 6-12 months is ideal.

Do you require a deposit?▶

Yes, a 50% deposit is required to secure your date and our equipment. The remaining balance is due on the day of the event.

Can you work with our budget?▶

We'll do our best. Tell us what you're working with in the event description, and we can discuss options or alternative packages that might fit your needs.
