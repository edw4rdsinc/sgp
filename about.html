FROM PERFORMER TO PRODUCER

Every sound engineer has a story. Mine starts on stage.

I spent years as a musician, chasing that perfect moment when everything clicks – the crowd connects, the band locks in, and you feel larger than life. I know what it's like to step on stage hoping the sound tech understands your vision. I know the frustration when they don't.

That experience drives everything we do at Soundguy Productions. When I started this company 20 years ago, I made a simple promise: every performer deserves to feel like a rockstar, and every audience deserves to hear them clearly.

Today, we're the trusted sound partner for venues, festivals, and events across the Pacific Northwest. From intimate coffee shop sets to 2,500-person summer concerts, we bring the same dedication to making everyone sound their best.

It's not just about the equipment. It's about understanding what artists need to feel confident, what venues need to run smoothly, and what audiences deserve to experience.

After 20 years, this isn't just my job – it's my passion.

- Rich, Founder

"I became a sound engineer because I wanted every performer to feel what I felt on stage – that magical moment when everything sounds perfect."
