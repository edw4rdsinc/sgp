Common Questions

How far in advance should I book? ▶

For best availability, we recommend booking 2-3 months in advance, especially for weekend dates. For large festivals, 6-12 months is ideal. However, don't hesitate to reach out for last-minute needs – we may have availability!

Do you provide lighting too? ▶

While our primary focus is audio, we offer lighting coordination as part of our Club and Festival packages. We work with trusted local lighting professionals to provide a seamless production experience.

Can you work with our venue's sound restrictions? ▶

Absolutely. Great sound doesn't have to be loud. We are experienced in working within noise ordinances and venue-specific SPL (Sound Pressure Level) limits. We use advanced tools to monitor levels and ensure compliance while still delivering a great-sounding show.

What if we need to cancel? ▶

Our cancellation policy will be clearly outlined in your agreement. Generally, the initial deposit is non-refundable, but we offer flexibility for rescheduling based on availability.

Do you have insurance? ▶

Yes, Soundguy Productions carries comprehensive liability insurance. We can provide a certificate of insurance (COI) to your venue upon request.
